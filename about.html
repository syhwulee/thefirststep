About Us

Samantha L. 

I am a junior at Wayland High School in Massachusetts. In 2022, I had the idea to create a website called The First Step, inspired by my personal experiences with mental health. I recognized the need for a support system that was anonymous and accessible to all. With the help of some close friends, I developed The First Step as a platform for teenagers to find resources and support in their recovery journey, as well as a place to share their stories and express themselves freely. My goal is to raise awareness and create a safe and welcoming community for teenagers.
