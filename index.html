Our Mission

Based in Wayland, MA, The First Step is an organization founded and run by teenagers dedicated to supporting other youth struggling with mental health. As teenagers who have also experienced challenges with mental health and the stigma around it, The First Step is uniquely positioned and capable of providing support and assistance to other teenagers while working to de-stigmatize mental health. Throughout our website and its various features, visitors will find valuable resources catered to teens designed to help them take the first step to recovery.
