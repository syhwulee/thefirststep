Mental Health

What is Mental Health?

Mental health refers to a person's overall psychological well-being. It includes the ability to manage one's emotions, thoughts, and behaviors, and to handle the stresses and challenges of daily life. Good mental health is essential for overall health and happiness, and it can affect everything from relationships and work to physical health and quality of life.

What is Mental Illness?

Mental health conditions, also known as mental illnesses or disorders, are conditions that can affect a person's mood, thinking, and behavior. Some common mental health conditions include depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, and eating disorders. These conditions can range from mild to severe, and they can be managed with treatment, including therapy, medication, and lifestyle changes.

Important Note

It is important to prioritize mental health and seek help if you are struggling with your mental health. It is also important to recognize that mental health can vary from person to person and can change over time. What is healthy for one person may not be healthy for another, and it is important to find what works best for you.
